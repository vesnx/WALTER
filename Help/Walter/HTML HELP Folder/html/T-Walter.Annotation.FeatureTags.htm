FeatureTags Enumeration

Enumerates the various feature flags associated with classes to categorize their functionalities and capabilities. These flags serve as markers to identify and discover classes based on their specialized roles within an application, such as parsing, encryption, validation, and more, facilitating a modular and feature-focused architecture.

Remarks

Feature flags help in aggregating and highlighting the key functionalities of a class, making it easier to understand the scope and utility of a class at a glance. They are particularly useful in large codebases where discovering and understanding the purpose of each class can be challenging. Classes, especially those implementing extension methods, may carry multiple feature flags to denote their applicability across various domains and use cases. This approach encourages a more organized and searchable documentation and codebase, enabling developers to quickly find the tools and functionalities they need to implement or extend their applications efficiently.
